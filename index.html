About walkies

Do you get bored walking your dog in the same place everyday? Do you know of all the dog friendly places in your area? Do you wish you could meet other dog loving people and their pets?

Walkies is an app to help you find new and interesting places to walk your dogs. With a range of locations scattered around Dublin you're sure to find a new place to suit you!
